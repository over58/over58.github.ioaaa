Advanceed Range Syntax

  • 1.2.3 - 2.3.4 := >=1.2.3 <=2.3.4
  • 1.2.3 - 2.3 := >=1.2.3 <2.4.0
  • 1.2.3 - 2 := >=1.2.3 <3.0.0

X-Ranges

    • := >=0.0.0 (Any version satisfies)
  • 1.x := >=1.0.0 <2.0.0 (Matching major version)
  • 1.2.x := >=1.2.0 <1.3.0 (Matching major and minor versions)
